Zip it!

Show all entries of a series in one item.

Simply cut the text to leave the common bit in every title. For example: Etho Plays Minecraft Episode 945: Redstone Black Hole would be cut down to Etho Plays Minecraft to catch all new entries to the series.

You can also choose whether the zip should apply to all channels or just the one that you were selecting a video from.

If you want to change filters later on, you can do that in the Settings. Also note that you will only be able to click and watch the latest entry in a zip.
